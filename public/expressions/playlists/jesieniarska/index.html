Fall is my favourite season, not least because the mood outside fits most of my music collection.

I created this playlist on a sunny autumn day and a less sunny afternoon, collecting music I had been enjoying in autumn for the past 5 years or so.

The playlist is not "curated" or "composed" or anything of that kind. It is more of a log of what music I was revisiting that day. Progressions of songs are genuine first thoughts even if they don't make much sense.

A note on the name: it means "the autumn-fan playlist", mixing Swedish and Polish words. It's a small word play exercising similarities of the declension of adjectives between these languages.
